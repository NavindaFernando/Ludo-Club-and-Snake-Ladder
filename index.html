Rules

Beginning the Game :

1. Decide who goes first by click the guess board.
2. Roll a 6 to bring your first pawn onto the board.
3. Roll again to determine how many spaces to move your first pawn.
4. End your turn and pass the dice to the next player after you move a pawn.
5. Place another pawn on the board if you roll another 6.

Moving Pawns Around the Board :

6. Continue to roll at the beginning of each turn and move your pawns.
7. Capture an opponents pawn by landing on the same space.
8. Move your pawns around the board going towards your home stretch.

Winning the Game :

9. Roll the exact number needed to get each pawn onto the home base.
10. Reach the home space with all 4 of your pawns first to win the game.
